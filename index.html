Timber Trainer VR

A virtual reality training system for new forestry operators to safely learn and practice harvester skills.

Modern forestry operations rely on specialized machinery, which requires a high level of skill and experience to operate safely and efficiently. However, traditional harvester training requires expensive equipment and carries safety risks for new operators.

These challenges create barriers to entry and slow training. Timber Trainer VR uses virtual reality to simulate real-world tasks, allowing trainees to learn controls and practice skills without the same cost or risk.

Why Timber Trainer?

  • Train safely without real-world risk
  • Reduce cost of training equipment
  • Repeat scenarios to build confidence
  • Accessible on standalone VR hardware
